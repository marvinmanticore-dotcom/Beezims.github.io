As I was looking through some more dinosaur models I started thinking about the phenomenon of people grieving lost media. Stories that are fundamentally changed (either from writer's choice or more often pressured from censorship applied by businesses) once discovered are mourned. I think it's just interesting (as well as a huge fear of mine as a writer) that people miss stories they haven't heard.
